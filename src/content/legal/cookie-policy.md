# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: 15th June, 2026** · Version 1.0

This Cookie Policy explains how **KHIJANI COGNITIVE SYSTEMS LIMITED** ("Khijani") uses cookies and
similar technologies across the Khijani platform — the marketing website, the teacher/admin portal, and the student app. It should be read together with our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

---

## 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar
technologies (such as local storage and pixels) work in comparable ways. They help
sites function, remember preferences, keep you signed in, and understand usage.

## 2. How we use them, by surface

### Marketing website ([WEBSITE URL])
- **Strictly necessary** — for core site functionality and security.
- **Analytics** (if enabled) — to understand how visitors use the site so we can
  improve it.

### Teacher/admin portal ([PORTAL URL])
- **Strictly necessary** — authentication and session cookies that keep teachers and
  administrators securely signed in and the portal working. These cannot be switched
  off without breaking the service.

### Student app ([APP URL])
- **Strictly necessary only** — the app uses only what is needed for it to function
  (e.g. maintaining a session during an activity). We do **not** use advertising or
  cross-site tracking cookies in the student app.

## 3. Categories of cookies we may use

| Category | Purpose | Can you turn it off? |
|---|---|---|
| Strictly necessary | Security, authentication, core functionality | No — the service won't work without them |
| Functional | Remembering preferences | Yes, via browser settings |
| Analytics | Understanding aggregate usage | Yes, where a consent control is provided |

We do **not** use cookies for behavioural advertising, and we do **not** sell data
collected via cookies.

## 4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by trusted service providers acting on our behalf (for
example, hosting or analytics), as described in our
[Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

## 5. Your choices

- **Browser settings** — most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking
  strictly necessary cookies will stop parts of the Platform working.
- **Consent controls** — where we use non-essential cookies (e.g. analytics on the
  website), we will provide a way to accept or decline them where required by law.

## 6. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy as our use of cookies changes. The current version
applies from its "Last updated" date.
